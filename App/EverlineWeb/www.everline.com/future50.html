Everline Future 50

in partnership with Real Business

We've partnered with Real Business to celebrate the Everline Future 50 - Britian's most disruptive early-stage businesses who are challenging the traditional way of doing things in their field. We're really proud to support and celebrate the achievements of these businesses and wish them every success in the future.

  • Kabbee

    Kabbee is an app that compares local cab firms to get the best value for the journey you are taking. In November 2013, Kabbee closed a £3.8m funding round with a view to accelerating its growth in London and beyond.

  • Movebubble

    Movebubble is the world's first peer-to-peer community marketplace for long term residential property lettings. Founded in 2013 on the premise that moving should be easy, it enables renters, owners and contractors to collaborate removing the need for an agent. It also allows users to move, manage and maintain homes from anywhere in the world, on any device, offering flexibility & value for money

  • Liftshare.com

    Liftshare.com pioneered the idea of sharing car journeys back in 1998. Now with more than 100,000 people sharing every day, it is one of the most successful journey-matching services in the world. In early 2013 the company launched its new online personal travel planning tool, myPTP, which offers tailored journey-planning advice using all modes of transport, including park & ride and car sharing. Already Liftshare has sold 70,000 myPTP travel plans to public and private organisations.

  • Reevoo

    Reevoo deals with the issue that trusted reviews are a powerful force in ecommerce but are not always credibly delivered. The company has pioneered a new approach to collecting, moderating and displaying reviews with an invitation-only solution that provides greater returns and eliminates any chance of brand damage from fake reviews

  • Tabl.com

    Tabl.com is disrupting the traditional hospitality sector by offering a peer-to-peer social dining platform that connects host and guest users, turning private houses, apartments and other spaces into informal, pop-up eating establishments. Tabl delivers secure payment handling, reservation management, messaging systems, social media and marketing, allowing its host community to focus on culinary skills and hospitality without the overheads and commitments of a permanent restaurant. Tabl.com goes live in April 2014.

  • CompareTheCoffin

    CompareTheCoffin.com aims to change the way the UK plans and organises funerals. Everything in life can be compared online – why not funerals? Comparethecoffin.com offers a Coffin Shop with prices less than half those charged by most funeral directors and free planning tools to create a funeral plan for yourself, and also a plan that is needed right now. This ‘immediate’ plan is sent to 100’s of funeral directors who ‘bid’ for the business. Comparing Funeral Directors is hard, with less than 1 in 10 people going to more than one FD, Comparethecoffin.com makes it easy and puts you in control

  • Asset Match

    Asset Match is the first comprehensive electronic platform in the UK for trading shares in private companies. It helps UK private companies provide liquidity for their shareholders and, importantly, lets the company control who buys, who sells and when. Established in 2012 and FCA regulated, Asset Match offers three different types of ‘Market’ - Open, Monitored, and Closed. This, coupled with a variable calendar (quarterly, monthly, weekly) for share transactions, allows investors a facility to access liquidity in their shares, without the distractions, regulations and high costs of a Stock Exchange listing

  • Appearhere

    Appearhere is a marketplace for landlords to find tenants for commercial property. The company has raised more than £1.5m to date and recently signed exclusives with London Underground and some of the UK's top property companies
