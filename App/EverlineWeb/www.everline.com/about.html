Putting you in the driver’s seat

At Everline we know cashflow is vital when opportunities come along. We provide loans to fuel business growth.

  • It takes money to make money

At Everline, we know that it takes money to make money

Everline is a leading alternative provider of working capital to small businesses. We offer customers a simple, frictionless and transparent process. Using innovative technology, data and our partnerships, we make informed and responsible lending decisions.

Limited companies and limited liability partnerships can borrow between £3,000 and £50,000 for up to 52 weeks. We make clear to applicants the cost of our loans at the start of the process. If an application has been approved, funds are sent within five minutes. Customers can even repay early, in full or in part, at any time at no extra cost. This saves customers money as they are only charged interest for the time they have borrowed the funds.

Interest is fixed at 0.5% per week which equates to an interest cost of 26% for a 52 week loan. An arrangement fee of 1.5% of the loan amount is also payable (the arrangement fee does not accrue interest).

Meet Russel, Everline's MD

Russell Gould

Managing director

With a strong business background in both start-up and corporate environments, Russell understands the challenges faced by small businesses. Along with the Everline team, he aims to overcome working capital challenges, by providing small business owners with simple and frictionless access to funds.

Before launching Everline he ran his own consulting business and has held senior roles across the technology, travel, insurance and banking sectors.
