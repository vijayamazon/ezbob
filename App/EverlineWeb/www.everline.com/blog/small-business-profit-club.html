Small business profit club

25th June 2013 - Last updated at 11:21

Our friends at SmallBusiness.co.uk are launching a new initiative called The Small Business Profit Club.

The new service is an advice and support network for the UK’s 4.5 million small businesses. The club will deliver a wealth of benefits to help owners, managers and ambitious sole traders achieve success and a sustained profit for growth. The Small Business Profit Club will include the following…

  • A priority Q&A service – access to a panel of 30 industry experts answering member’s questions and delivering support
  • Savings and business offers – exclusive business deals for owner-managers starting, running and growing a business
  • Events– regional events discussing key topics. Open Q&A forum with experts and lead sponsors
  • Lobbying – campaigning for small businesses and pushing their ideas for government change
  • Community– connect and share ideas and success stories with like-minded individuals  
  • Business know how – indepth how to guides and featured research   

SmallBusiness.co.uk has always been a great source of free content for UK SMEs so it’ll be interesting to see how their premium content develops. It looks like an interesting service and one we’ll be following closely.
